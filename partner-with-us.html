Become a Sponsor

  1. Home
  2. Become a Sponsor

InfraGard San Francisco Bay Area Members Alliance is a California, public benefit, FBI affiliated 501(c)(3) nonprofit corporation managed by an elected Board of Directors. Your generious sponsorship and financial support is tax deductable and enables our non-profit organization to provide essential information sharing, training, collaboration, and support services to hundreds of companies in the Bay Area.

Our Board of Directors and Operating Committee are comprised of industry leaders from Bay Area companies. This group represents multiple critical infrastructure sectors and sets the strategic plan for the San Francisco Bay Area Chapter.

InfraGard events are premier gatherings for professionals across various sectors. Our events are attended by upwards of 150 representatives from multiple federal agencies, law enforcement, and corporate executives and security.

The San Francisco Bay Area Chapter has a high traffic website, hold an average of 6 in-person events per year, and offers various training and collaboration opportunities to our members and sponsors. Our newsletter is promoted and distributed to members, non-members, and sponsors in our ever-growing chapter.



In addition to Sponsorship Levels listed above, you may consider supporting us for:

  • • Individual Sponsorship – We are a 100% volunteer-run organization and your donation in any amount will help support our year-round activities. Your donations maybe matched by your employer.
  • • Event Sponsorship – sponsor one or more of the key needs below for our Chapter Meeting:
    • - Facility and/or Food/drinks
    • - Cost for the speaker/travel expenses
  • • Service Sponsorship - sponsor one or more of the key services below for our Chapter Meeting:
    • - Video production, Training
    • - Web development, Administration

Your financial support is greatly appreciated. For questions regarding sponsorship, please contact InfraGard Sponsorship Chair John Doe at info@sfbay-infragard.org.


Donating to our chapter is as easy as pressing the "Donate" button located in the upper right hand corner of our website. For mobile users, the button can be located after pressing the 3 bar icon on the upper right hand corner. Donations sent to our non-profit Paypal business account is deducted 2.2% by Paypal + $0.30 per transaction with no monthly fees.
