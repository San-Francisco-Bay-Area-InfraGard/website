About Donnie

  1. Home

Donnie Hasseltine is the Infragard Bay Area Sector Chief for the Information Technology Sector. He is the Chief Security Officer for Xenon Partners, a tech private equity firm that specializes in B2B SaaS companies. Prior to Xenon, he served as a U.S. Marine Corps officer in the infantry and reconnaissance fields.
