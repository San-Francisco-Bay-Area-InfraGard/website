San Francisco Bay Area InfraGard Members Alliance

The InfraGard San Francisco Bay Area Member Alliance is an FBI-affiliated 501(c)(3) non-profit organization serving as a public-private partnership among U.S. businesses, individuals involved in the protection and resilience of U.S. Critical Infrastructure, and the Federal Bureau of Investigation.


OUR MISSION:

To facilitate the sharing of information and best practices among members. To enable proactive and reliable bi-directional communications on critical infrastructure protection between the Bay Area industry and government. To provide a program of activities (meetings, conferences, seminars) for our members and other public and private sector partners that will foster education, information gathering and dissemination, and the development of analytical products.
