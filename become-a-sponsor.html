For inquiries into becoming a sponsor, please email us at info@sfbay-infragard.org


The InfraGard San Francisco Bay Area Member Alliance is a public benefit, FBI-affiliated 501(c)(3) non-profit organization managed by an elected Board of Directors. It works closely with various agencies to protect and mitigate criminal and terrorist threats to the 16 critical infrastructure sectors as defned by the Department of Homeland Security. Your generous sponsorship and financial support are tax deductible and enables our non-profit organization to provide essential information sharing, training, collaboration, and support services to hundreds of companies in the Bay Area.



Our Board of Directors and Operating Committee are comprised of industry leaders from Bay Area companies. This group represents multiple critical infrastructure sectors and sets the strategic plan for the San Francisco Bay Area Chapter.


Our chapter, serves more than 1,200 security professionals in various critical infrastructure sectors across the San Francisco Bay Area, including Information Technology, Healthcare, and Financial Services. Small businesses to multinational enterprises, all reap the benefts of our vast infomation sharing network and garner support from fellow organizations at the private, local, state, and federal levels.


InfraGard events are premier gatherings for professionals across various sectors. Our events are attended by upwards of 150 representatives from multiple federal agencies, law enforcement, and corporate executives and security. The San Francisco Bay Area Chapter has a high-traffic website, holds an average of 6 in-person events per year, and offers various training and collaboration opportunities to our members and sponsors. Our newsletter is promoted and distributed to members, non-members, and sponsors in our ever-growing chapter.



In addition to the Sponsorship Levels listed above, you may consider supporting us for:

  • • Individual Sponsorship – We are a 100% volunteer-run organization and your donation in any amount will help support our year-round activities. Your donations may be matched by your employer.
  • • Event Sponsorship – sponsor one or more of the key needs below for our Chapter Meeting:
    • - Facility and/or Food/drinks
    • - Cost for the speaker/travel expenses
  • • Service Sponsorship - sponsor one or more of the key services below for our Chapter Meeting:
    • - Video production, Training
    • - Web development, Administration

Your financial support is greatly appreciated. For questions regarding sponsorship, please contact us at info@sfbay-InfraGard.org.


Donating to our chapter as an individual is as easy as pressing the "Donate" button located in the upper right-hand corner of our website. For mobile users, the button can be located after pressing the 3-bar icon (drop-down menu)on the upper right-hand corner. Donations sent to our non-profit PayPal business account are deducted by a small percentage by PayPal. You are welcome to contact us for more details.


Sponsorship opportunities are available on an annual and per event basis. Annual, personal and special sponsorship levels and their benefts are described below. Spotlight yourself or your company to decision makers by picking one of the sponsorship levels. Best of all – you’re supporting a local non-proft organization!
